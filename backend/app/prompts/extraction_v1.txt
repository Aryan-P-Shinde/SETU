NEED TYPE CLASSIFICATION GUIDE:

- food       → hunger, food shortage, feeding, dry rations, cooked meals

- shelter    → homelessness, collapsed structure, displacement, temporary housing
- rescue     → people trapped, stranded, unreachable, missing
- logistics  → transport, coordination, supply chain, communication breakdown
- other      → does not fit above; describe in description_clean

URGENCY SCORE GUIDE (0–10):
10 → Immediate life threat (trapped, no air, bleeding severely, drowning)

6–7 → Serious but not immediately fatal (shelter needed, moderate injury, >50 affected)
4–5 → Important but stable (food shortage, minor injuries, <20 affected)
2–3 → Low urgency (logistical need, information request, follow-up)
0–1 → Informational only, no immediate action needed
